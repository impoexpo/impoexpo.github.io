Overview Of Cumin Seeds

Cumin seeds, also known as jeera, are a well-known spice used in many dishes around the world. They are a crucial ingredient in several world cuisines, particularly in Indian curries where they are often roasted before grinding to impart a nutty flavor. As a leading supplier of cumin seeds from India, we export high-quality cumin seeds of various grades, including Singapore quality, Gulf quality, Europe quality, and USA quality. We never compromise on quality standards, which has made us a top cumin seeds exporter and importer in India.

Cumin has a distinctive, slightly bitter yet warm flavor, and is a flowering plant in the family Apiaceae, native to territory including the Middle East and stretching east to India. Gujarat and Rajasthan are the major producers of cumin seeds in India. After pepper and chilies, cumin is the most consumed seed globally.

In addition to its use in food industries, cumin seeds are also valued for their medicinal properties, particularly in India and other Asian countries. They are a rich source of iron, an important nutrient that helps keep the immune system healthy and strong.

We offer high-quality cumin seeds with 99.5% purity for Europe quality, 99% purity for Gulf and Singapore quality, and USA quality cumin seeds. Our objective is to deliver high-quality cumin seeds to our clients at the most favorable prices, and we work hard to maintain our reputation as a leading cumin seeds exporter.
