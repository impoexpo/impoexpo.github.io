01 How old is Bhagwati World in this industry? 

Bhagwati World has been into research for a very long time. We have been serving in the water industry for around 20 years, with our international offices based out of the US and Australia as well.

02 How does Bhagwati World water conditioner work?

Bhagwati World water conditioner utilizes a unique formulation of 26 types of noble and semi-noble metals to facilitate a redox (reduction-oxidation) reaction. This process alters the molecular structure of hard water, making it softer without removing any minerals.

03 Is a lab report necessary?

No lab report is needed. Our product is effective with TDS and hardness levels up to 40,000. It's a proven technology that doesn't rely on conventional water quality metrics.

04 Is the conditioned water safe to drink

Yes, the water is safe to drink. The conditioning process produces antioxidant water that is not harmful to your body.
